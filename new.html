Dev Festival 2025

Dear Poornimites, under the banner of Spic Macay and Desi Kalakaarz Club, Poornima Institute of Engineering & Technology invites girl students of First and Second Year to participate in Dev Festival 2025, organized by Jawahar Kala Kendra, Jaipur.

The festival celebrates the life and legacy of the legendary actor Shri Dev Anand, with a special focus on Women Empowerment through art and culture.

Event details

  • Date: Friday, 26th September 2025
  • Time: 9:30 a.m. onwards
  • Venue: Rangayan Auditorium, Jawahar Kala Kendra, Jaipur

Opportunities

  • Musical Quiz Show on Dev Anand’s films and philosophy
  • Dance performances on Dev Anand’s songs
  • Attend in colorful festive attire to represent the institution with spirit and pride

Important

  • Attendance will be provided to all participants
  • Conveyance will be arranged by the college
